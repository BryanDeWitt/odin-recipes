Ingredients

  • 2kg of meat
  • 1kg of dough
  • 300grs of bacon
  • 5 hot dogs
  • Ham
  • Muzzarella
  • Sauce

How to prepare this divine dish?

  1. Cook the meat, add salt and all spices that you like
  2. Put the meat with the cheese in the floor of the dough
  3. Add the hot dogs in the borders and put it some bacon
  4. Put the ceiling of the dough and add some sauce
  5. Enjoy the dish of the GODS
